Team

Manchester City

English club Man City FC has been the best football team in the world in 2023. It continues to find new ways to win games of football. It's that simple. Manchester City's managers always find ways to adapt to challenges like finding a replacement for retired player Sergio Aguero. English football club Man City FC has been the best football team in the world in 2023.

Bayern Munich

Bayern has been one of the best football teams ever in the world. They have 32 Bundesliga titles, 20 DFB-Pokal titles, two Champions League trophies, and more. FC Bayern Munich has maintained a consistent force in football. They have the world's best striker, Robert Lewandowski, but he was overlooked for the Ballon d'Or in the 2021/2022 season after scoring 41 goals in 29 league games.

Liverpool

Liverpool FC won two European Cups (2005 and 2019) and got its 19th League title in 2020. It is the second on the list of teams with the most championships (19). Manchester United tops the list with 20 Premier League championships which it won from 1889 to 2020. Liverpool FC has played its home games at Anfield since its formation.

PSG

Paris Saint-Germain FC is the most successful club in French football history, having won more than 30 championships. They have won Ligue 1, a domestic French football competition, six times. The league features formidable opponents like Bordeaux, Monaco, Brest, Reims, and Marseille. PSG FC has players like Kylian Mbappe, Neymar, and Lionel Messi.

Chelsea

In 2012, Chelsea FC won their first Champions League championship, and in 2013, they won the Europa League. As a result, it became the first football team to double up on all four major European trophies. In addition, Chelsea FC is the only team in football history to have won all four major European trophies twice. The Blues are among the best football teams in the world, ranked number 5.

Real Madrid

Real Madrid's 35 La Liga championship titles are more than any other Spanish team has won. Additionally, the team has won two UEFA Cups, 19 Copa del Rey cups, 25 Copa Eva Duarte titles, and more. Players like Karim Benzema, Toni Kroos, and Luka Modric are its spine in La Liga and their Champions League. Real Madrid has been one of the best football teams in the world for a long time.

Inter Milan

FC Internazionale is undoubtedly one of the most adored clubs in Italy. Inter Milan won their 19th Serie A championship by defeating teams like Atalanta, AC Milan, and Juventus. After 11 long years, Inter finally won the Scudetto, depriving the Bianconeri of their chance to win the Serie A championship for the tenth time in a row.

Juventus

The club won 36 Serie A league titles, 14 Coppa Italia titles, 9 national Super Cup titles, 2 Intercontinental Cups, 2 European Cups/UEFA Champions Leagues, a national record, and more. Juventus FC is a historic giant with total dominance in Italy for the last decade.

Manchester United

In 1968, Manchester United became the first English team to triumph in the European Championship. They won the Triple in 1999, making them the first English club to do so. They won the FA Cup, Champions League, and Premier League crowns. Of course, the 2021/22 season did not pan out expectedly. Reports of discontent are never far away, but Man United is not in terminal decline.

Ajax

The Dutch side enjoyed a stellar run to the Champions League semi-finals in 2019. Unfortunately, that side was broken up and splintered across Europe, but highly-rated former boss Erik ten Hag rebuilt the squad and won all six Champions League group games during that term. Being in the less prestigious Dutch league, AFC Ajax has a lower ceiling than most teams on this list, but they remain a jewel in European football.

Players

Cristiano Ronaldo

Renowned footballer hailing from Portugal, born on February 5, 1985. A true legend known for his incredible skills, record-breaking goal-scoring, and multiple championships with Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Also a key figure in Portugal's national team triumphs, including the 2016 UEFA European Championship. Off the pitch, Ronaldo's charisma and philanthropy have made him a global icon.

Lionel Messi

Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Messi is a football prodigy celebrated for his extraordinary talent and records. Spending the majority of his career at Barcelona, Messi's unmatched dribbling, precise playmaking, and goal-scoring prowess have garnered him multiple Ballon d'Or awards. With Barça, he secured numerous La Liga titles and Champions League triumphs. In 2021, he moved to Paris Saint-Germain, continuing his legacy of greatness. On the international stage, Messi led Argentina to victory in the 2021 Copa America, solidifying his status as a football icon worldwide.

A dynamic attacker with remarkable dribbling, he's a vital asset to Brazil's national team, contributing to their 2019 Copa America win. Off the pitch, Neymar's charisma and global popularity make him a standout figure in the football world.

Rooney also represented England in multiple international tournaments, showcasing his commitment to the national team. Piqué rose through the ranks at Barcelona, becoming a pivotal part of their golden era with numerous La Liga and Champions League triumphs. His ability to read the game and contribute to build-up play made him a standout defender. Piqué also played a crucial role in Spain's national team successes, including their 2010 World Cup victory. His move to Barcelona solidified his status as one of the world's top strikers, winning La Liga and Champions League titles.
